Beauty has been my passion since I was young. For me, helping others feel confident and look their best isn't just a service — it's an experience rooted in education and intention.

I'm Juhi, a licensed esthetician with five years of hands-on experience in the beauty industry. Those years taught me something invaluable: everyone is different and unique, and they all need and desire different treatments. JuGlow was created with that understanding at its core.

My approach is education-led. I believe in building genuine connections with my clients, understanding their skin, and guiding them toward treatments that deliver long-term results — not quick fixes. Whether you're here for brow refinement, a restorative facial, or advanced skin treatments, my focus is always on you, your goals, and creating lasting change.

I want you to feel at ease, connected, and cared for. This is a space where warmth meets expertise, and where your beauty journey is truly yours.
